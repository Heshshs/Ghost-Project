About The Game

Ghost Project is a horror survival experience built around realistic ghost hunting mechanics.

Players explore haunted locations, gather supernatural evidence using advanced equipment, and survive encounters with intelligent spirits. Each ghost has unique behaviors, strengths, and weaknesses, making every mission unpredictable and intense.
